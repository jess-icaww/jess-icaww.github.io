    About Me

    Even as a child, I loved to learn. Whether it was memorizing magic tricks on Youtube, or making a whole cardboard arcade in my basement, I had a knack for trying out new things. I not only realized how far a brain and two hands could get me, but how beautiful, enriching, and transformative it was for me to try, no matter how many times it took me.

    If you were wondering where my love for technology comes from, you're gonna have to keep reading. Long story short, I used to be obsessed with playing video games. It was one of my biggest insecurities, but I realize how it interestingly enough, has turned into one of my biggest blessings. Why? Well... my love for the specific game, Minecraft, caused me to play nonstop. I eventually got really good at the game, broke a few world records, won competitive tournaments here and there, and decided to upload a few videos on my gameplay. Sooner or later, I started gaining traction and decided I wanted to take Youtube seriously. I self-taught myself different video and photo editing software, and ended up gaining over 200,000 subscribers on Youtube and 40,000 followers on Twitch.

    Cultivating an online community and bearing it's various responsibilities has taught me about the importance of skills like communication, storytelling, adaptability, collaboration, time-management, problem-solving, and creativity. But even more than this, I gained a reason and a purpose to use technology in hopes to provide value to people. Whether it's creating videos to writing code, I'm passionate about exploring tech and contributing in any way I can.
